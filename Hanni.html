Trivia

- Hanni is the first Vietnamese idol to debut under HYBE.
- Hanni was born in Australia to Vietnamese parents.
- She uses an Australian passport while traveling.
- Hanni speaks Vietnamese, English, and Korean.
- She speaks Vietnamese with a Northern accent and is not fully fluent.
- She speaks English with a Melbourne General Australian Accent.
- She has a younger sister named Jasmine.
- She loves bread.
- She plays the ukulele, guitar and piano.
- She is a 2nd degree black belt in taekwondo. She can only do a jumping front kick with the right leg as kicking leg.
- After her appearance in BTS's "Permission to Dance" MV, fans nicknamed her Cherry as they didn't know her name.
- She is one of the few Vietnamese idols in the K-pop industry.
- If she had gone to university instead of debuting as an idol, she would have liked to study science and medicine or filmography.
- Despite having cat-like visuals, she is a dog owner, unlike Minji and Danielle.
- She is the most competitive member in the group, but Minji trumps her in terms of win rates in both athletics and luck-based games.
