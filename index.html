About NewJeans

NewJeans ( 뉴진스 ) is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR.
The group is composed of five members : Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin and Hyein. NewJeans is known for their girl next door image and 1990s- and 2000s-indebted pop and R&B songs with influences of various dance and club styles.

NewJeans Debut

On July 1, the first teaser videos were released and it was also announced that the group would release their first content on the 22nd.
On July 22, the group released the debut music video for their lead single "Attention" and the debut schedule for their first EP, New Jeans.
The music videos for "Hype Boy" and "Hurt" were released on July 23 and July 25, respectively. ADOR announced that as of July 27,
New Jeans had surpassed 444,000 stock pre-orders, the highest number achieved by any girl group debut album in history.
On August 1, the group's debut EP was released digitally, along with the music video for "Cookie." NewJeans then held their debut stage
on the August 4 broadcast of Mnet's M! Countdown, performing three of their tracks, "Attention", "Hype Boy", and "Cookie."
On August 18, the group took their first-ever music show win on the August 18 broadcast of M! Countdown.
